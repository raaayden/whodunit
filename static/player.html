❓ How to Play

3 rounds. Talk in person. Check your phone for clues. Vote for the killer at the end.

🎯 Win Conditions
  • Innocents win — majority of living innocents vote for the real killer.
  • Killer wins — killer avoids getting the majority vote.
  • Jester wins — if the Jester tricks the majority into voting for them, everyone else loses. Neither innocents nor killer win.
🃏 Roles
  • 😇 Innocent — share clues, build cases, vote correctly.
  • 🔪 Killer — blend in, secretly queue one elimination per round. You do not know your accomplice.
  • 🤝 Accomplice — you know the killer. Protect them and spread disinformation.
  • ☠️ Poisoner — an accomplice variant. Pick one player each round on your device — their next clue is silently corrupted. They never know.
  • 🔎 Investigator — your Round 3 clue names exactly two suspects. One is the killer.
  • 🍺 Drunk — you believe you are innocent. All your clues are false. You have no way of knowing.
  • 🧠 Paranoid — your gut tells you a specific person is the killer. It is pointing at an innocent. Trust it anyway.
  • 🕵️ Spy — once per game, secretly learn one other player's true role via your device.
  • 🃏 Fool — you think you are the killer but you are not. Play your role sheet as written. You are innocent.
  • 🎭 Jester — you are neither killer nor innocent. Your only goal is to get the majority to vote for YOU. Act suspicious. Be theatrical. Make them think you did it.
  • ⚰️ Undertaker — after Round 2's murder is announced, you privately learn the victim's true role on your device.
  • 🎭 Recluse — you are innocent, but every detection ability reads you as the killer. You will never be told this is happening.
  • 📋 Alibi Cards — every player has a private alibi for the time of the murder. Only the killer's (and accomplices') are false. Share yours or keep it hidden.
💀 Eliminated?
  • You can still talk in person — use that.
  • You lose your final vote (unless ghost vote applies).
  • Send one ghost clue to a living player's device.
⚖️ Exile Vote (Round 2)
  • Living players can nominate someone for exile — a public removal before the final round.
  • If the majority agree, that player is exiled. Exiling an innocent helps the killer.
⚡ Crisis Dilemma (special games)
  • At Round 2, everyone votes on a critical decision.
  • If the dangerous option wins, the killer gets an extra elimination before Round 3.
