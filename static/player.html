📝 How to Play

  • Innocents: Speak to others, share your clues, and vote for the Killer. At least 50% of living Innocents must guess correctly to win.
  • The Killer: Deflect blame, lie, and secretly queue 1 player to eliminate per round.
  • Eliminations: If you die, you lose your vote. However, you can send ONE final "Ghost Clue" to a living player's device.
