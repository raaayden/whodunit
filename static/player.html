❓ How to Play
  • Innocents — share clues, argue your case, and vote for who you think the killer is. At least half of living innocents must vote correctly to win.
  • Killer — blend in, deflect suspicion, and secretly queue one player to eliminate each round.
  • Accomplice — you know the killer's identity. Protect them, spread false leads.
  • Poisoner — each round, pick one player on your device. Their next clue will be silently corrupted.
  • Investigator — your Round 2 clue gives you a 50/50 ping: one of two named players is the killer.
  • Drunk — you think you're innocent, but all your clues are false.
  • Paranoid — your gut points at a specific person. Trust it completely (even if it's wrong).
  • Eliminated? — you can still talk, but lose your vote. Send one ghost clue to a living player's device.
